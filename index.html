§ 02 Monthly Analysis
February showed consistent staffing gaps on voice across all POD queues, while chat and callback channels performed significantly better — suggesting members are successfully self-routing to alternative channels when voice wait times are unacceptable.

Voice remains the critical gap. With a 51% answer rate and 20.8% abandon rate across 2,304 offered contacts, nearly half of all voice contacts went unanswered. All four POD voice queues fell below 19% SL against an 80% target — a pattern suggesting systemic under-staffing or routing misconfiguration rather than isolated incidents.

Chat is performing well on volume but poorly on SL. 85.1% of 1,295 chat contacts were answered, but SL ranges from 0.3% (POD2) to 35.4% (POD1), with handle times of 1–2+ hours — pointing to agent multitasking or complex issue resolution in the chat channel.

Callback is the standout channel at 98.9% completion. While wait times for callbacks are long (3h 12m average), members who choose this path are nearly always reached — a positive member experience story worth highlighting.
