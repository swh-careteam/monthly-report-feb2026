§ 02 Weekly Analysis
This week shows flat voice performance with a major chat surge — overall answer rates held near last week while chat volume jumped 38%, CE Team voice finally crossed 80%, and POD3 chat ACW anomaly continues unresolved.

Voice performance remained essentially flat week-over-week. Of 563 offered contacts, 305 were answered (54.2%) — nearly identical to last week's 55.1%. The abandon rate improved from 17.1% to 14.9%. Direct Routing accounted for 162 of the 563 offered contacts with only 41 answered (25.3%), reflecting the direct-to-agent routing model rather than a true miss. CE Team Voice posted an 82.3% answer rate — the first queue to break 80% this reporting period.

Chat volume surged 37.9% with stable quality. Chat contacts jumped from 195 to 269 offered this week. All four PODs maintained answer rates between 85–95%, though no queue reached the 80% SL threshold (best: POD1 at 66.0%). The POD3 chat ACW anomaly persists at 1h 23m 33s — now in its second consecutive week. POD4 chat ACW at 22m 29s remains elevated. Both queues need wrap-up state auditing.

Telemedica showed continued partial recovery but remains critical. With 65 voice contacts and 32 answered (49.2%), Telemedica improved from last week's 33.7% answer rate but still sits far below the 80% target. The 27.7% abandon rate — more than 1 in 4 callers — remains unacceptable and unchanged from the prior week. Telemedica also received 15 callbacks this week, completing 16 (106.7%, including carryover), but the avg callback wait of 8h 17m is extremely elevated and requires immediate investigation.
